   on reality?" but "what rules does reality impose on this instance of
  humanity?" Governance here means: the set of non-negotiable constraints 
  bounding the possibility-space of human existence as instantiated in 2026.

  Every human project — political, technological, spiritual — operates inside

  formalization is itself governed by the very rules it formalizes. The map is
  inside the territory.
