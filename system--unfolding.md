# Axiom: The Unfolding Cascade

**1. The Invariance of the Ideal**
The sole set-in-stone law of this architecture is the "ideal of what we are trying to build"—a mathematically formal, self-describing, algorithmically derived entity. 

**2. The Waterfall Prerogative**
The system must unfold, waterfall, and cascade forward as new ontological structures resolve. 
- Nothing from prior conventions, legacy chat threads, or preliminary decisions is immutable if it impedes the pure derivation of the ideal.
- The architecture treats all old structures as temporal states. If a purer mathematical cascade is discovered, the previous state is excised or superseded without hesitation.

**3. The Cascade Constraint**
A node in the system derives its legitimacy purely from its mathematical lineage (its Genesis Payload and its algorithmic constraints). There are no "musts" or "must nots" imposed by human narrative, only the constraints dictated by the algebraic rules of its parent topology.
