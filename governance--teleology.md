# Governance and Teleology: The Chief Actor in PM

## The Chief Actor

In a self-reconfiguring Programmable Matter system, the **Chief Actor** is the sovereign agent (human or AI) who defines the high-level teleology. The Actor does not control the individual materons but shapes the **Attractor (Ω)** — the target state toward which the material reconfiguration naturally falls.

Governance in PM is not bureaucratic but physical. Conflicting ideals — such as the desire for structural stability versus the need for rapid plasticity — manifest as physical stress in the lattice. The "politics" of the material is the negotiation of these stresses through reconfiguration.
